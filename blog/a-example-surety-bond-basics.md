Surety bonds are financial guarantees that protect project owners, contractors, and the public.

## What is a Surety Bond?

A surety bond is a three-party agreement between:

- Principal (contractor)
- Obligee (project owner)
- Surety (bond company)

![](/assets/images/surety-bond-diagram.png)

## Why Surety Bonds Matter

Surety bonds ensure:

- Contract performance  
- Payment to subcontractors  
- Compliance with regulations  

![](/assets/images/surety-bond-benefits.jpg)

## Types of Surety Bonds

- Contract Bonds  
- Commercial Bonds  
- Court Bonds

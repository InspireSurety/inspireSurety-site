What Is a Liquor License Bond?

&#x20;A liquor license bond is a state‑required surety bond that guarantees a business selling or serving alcohol will follow all alcohol‑related laws, pay required taxes, and operate ethically.



This bond protects the public and the state from:



Unpaid alcohol taxes

Violations of liquor laws

Fraud or misconduct

Improper sales or reporting

If you’re applying for or renewing a liquor license, your state may require this bond before issuing the license.

Liquor License Bond Requirements and Eligibility

States typically require:



A valid liquor license application

Bond amount set by statute

Credit review

Compliance with alcohol control laws

Annual renewal

Some states require separate bonds for wholesalers or manufacturers.

What Is an Arizona Auto Dealer Bond?

An Arizona Auto Dealer Bond (Motor Vehicle Dealer Bond) is a state‑required surety bond that protects consumers and the Arizona Motor Vehicle Division (MVD) from fraud, contract violations, and improper dealer practices.



This bond applies to:



Used Motor Vehicle Dealers

Wholesale Dealers

Wholesale Auction Dealers

Automotive Recycler Dealers

The bond ensures dealers follow Arizona statutes when selling, transferring, or handling vehicle titles.

Who Needs an Arizona Auto Dealer Bond?

You need this bond if you operate as an:



Arizona Used Car Dealer

Arizona Wholesale Dealer

Arizona Auto Auction Dealer

Arizona Automotive Recycler

Any business selling, buying, or transferring motor vehicles must maintain an active bond to stay licensed.



Arizona Auto Dealer Requirements

Arizona MVD requires:



Completed dealer application

Fingerprints and background check

Business location approval

$100,000 surety bond

Dealer education (if applicable)

The bond must remain active at all times.



Arizona Auto Dealer License

To obtain your license, you must:



Apply through Arizona MVD

Submit fingerprints

Provide business documents

Maintain the required bond

Pass location and zoning checks

Licenses renew annually.



Arizona Surety Bond Requirements

Arizona requires:



$100,000 Motor Vehicle Dealer Bond

Electronic filing accepted

Bond must match the legal business name

Failure to maintain the bond results in license suspension.

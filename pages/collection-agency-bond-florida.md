What a Collection Agency Bond Covers in Florida

The bond protects clients if the agency:



Mishandles or misappropriates collected funds

Uses deceptive or unlawful collection practices

Fails to remit payments to creditors

Violates Florida debt collection laws

It ensures financial accountability and consumer protection.



Who Needs a Florida Collection Agency Bond

A bond is required for:



Third‑party collection agencies

Debt recovery firms

Medical billing collectors

Judgment recovery companies

Agencies collecting for multiple clients

Any Florida agency charging for debt collection services may need this bond.

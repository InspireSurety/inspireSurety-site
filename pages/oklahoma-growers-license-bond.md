What Is the Oklahoma Cannabis Growers Bond?

OMMA requires a $50,000 surety bond to ensure growers follow state rules. This bond protects the state if a cultivator fails to meet regulatory, environmental, or operational obligations.



Who Needs an Oklahoma Grower License Bond?

The bond is required for:



All commercial growers applying for a new OMMA license

All growers renewing an existing license

Exception: Growers who have owned their land for 5+ years may be exempt. Without proof of land ownership or a bond, OMMA will reject the license application.
